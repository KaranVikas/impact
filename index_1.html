The work, initiative by initiative

Every program below is real, hands-on community work — coordinated end-to-end, from logistics to families to follow-through.

Promoting school admissions for slum children

Advocating for a better tomorrow

Worked with authorities and parents to enrol slum children in school — gathering each child's background, assembling the documentation families needed, and convincing parents of the long-term value of education.

Education access

MNC exposure visits for underprivileged children

Career Catalysts: MNC exposure visits

Organised office visits to companies and the Indian School of Business so underprivileged children could meet employee role models, see real workplaces, and dream bigger about their futures.

Aspiration & mentorship

75 people uplifted for India's 75th Independence Day

75 lives for the 75th Independence Day

Identified families in need across Chandigarh slums and helped them access government schemes — creating UDID cards, arranging GRIID counseling, and gifting wheelchairs that gave children mobility for the first time.

Disability & inclusion
Featured · DD National
COVID-19 relief, recognised on national television

COVID-19 relief — featured on DD National

Provided free meals twice daily, registered senior citizens for vaccination, arranged hospital pick-and-drop, and ran awareness on COVID safety — keeping vulnerable communities fed, informed, and protected through a critical period. The effort was recognised on DD National News.

Crisis response
Bachpan: protecting children from harm

Bachpan — Save The Innocence

As Program Coordinator, delivered child-safety workshops teaching personal safety and good-touch / bad-touch awareness — leading on-ground execution, session logistics, and partnership coordination between organisations.

Child safety
Creative learning & origami workshops

Creative education & engagement

Ran hands-on origami workshops designed to build creativity, focus, and interactive learning — turning a classroom into a space where children stayed curious and engaged.

Inspiring Young Minds — ISB & Knack Global visits

Inspiring young minds

Arranged visits for children to institutions like the Indian School of Business and companies like Knack Global — opening doors to environments most of them had never seen, and showing them what's possible.

Every-weekend study & food drives
